About Us

Lynk Youth Initiative is a youth-led nonprofit dedicated to service, cultural understanding, and community connection.

Our Mission

Lynk Youth Initiative empowers young people to lead community service, build cross-cultural understanding, and advocate for overlooked voices by connecting youth, cultures, and communities locally and globally.

Meet Our Members

Our members come from different backgrounds, experiences, and communities, but we are united by a shared commitment to leadership, service, and making a meaningful impact.
